## Introduction
Project TMS (Tsunami Mitigation System) is an early-stage engineering research initiative aimed at designing, simulating, and evaluating innovative structural and computational concepts for coastal disaster mitigation.

## Objectives
- Develop engineering concepts to reduce tsunami impact.
- Use physics-based simulations and structural models.
- Explore practical, deployable mitigation solutions.
- Produce reproducible, open scientific documentation.

## Current Status
- Concept phase
- Computational and structural modelling under development
- Literature review ongoing
